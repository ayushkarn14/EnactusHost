Events

On the occasion of National Nutrition Week Enactus DDUC conducted an informative live session on NUTRITION 101 with DT. LAKSHITA JAIN. Dt. Lakshita is the founder of health clinic Nutr. She is constantly writing leading newspapers such as Times of India, HT city, Femina, Cosmopolitan, and many more. She is a diabetes educator, meat technologist and has been counseling people on PCOS, weight loss, right eating.
Enactus DDUC successfully conducted a very insightful and informative session on ‘Nutrition 101: Malnutrition and other eating disorders’ with clinical dietician and sports nutritionist Ms. Manisha Mehta, a food scientist, holds a certification in sports nutrition from California with 8+ years of experience.
Enactus DDUC hosted a two-day "Orientation Programme 2021", wherein we gathered a total audience of 500 students. During the first part of the program which was held under Deeksharambh, our Co-President Akanshi Sharma and Director of Project Execution Astha, gave insight into the world of Enactus.
Read more
On day two, a semi-formal event called 'Alumni Alcove' was held wherein Co-President Nitish Brahma and Director of Research & Development Divyanshi Garg were in conversation with the alumni of Enactus DDUC, Kartik Khatri (Ex-President and founding team member of Enactus DDUC) from the batch of 2015-18 and Ram Gupta (President for the tenure 2020-21) from the batch of 2019-22. The participants consisted of our core team, senior team members and freshers, who came together to learn about the rich history of Enactus DDUC and to gain motivation from the ones that have been through college life and have moved on to greater steps on their career paths.
Conducted an online 2-day event Nexus_22 which had a footfall of 800+ participants from all over Delhi University and increased the branch image by 40%. Nexus_22 was conducted to promote Enactus DDUC and its projects, primarily Project Mehsoos, and to be a source of joy during the tough times of the Covid-19 Pandemic.
